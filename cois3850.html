About the Project

As a part of the Project Management course's group project assignment, we decided to build a Website for New International students that guides them since they get the University offer letter until the first day of classes. As a group of 4, we came up with the project idea together considering the timeline & requirements as a University student. Our aim was to implement Agile Methodology and finish the project within the 8 weeks. Each Sprint was 2 weeks long, followed by the final presentation after the 8 weeks. We had 2 weeks for the initial planning and gathering requirements where we identified out project, met with the client and gathered requirements. Our project had a large scope which we narrowed down to the initial and most important steps to accommodate in the 8 week period.

We followed Agile, SCRUM and maintained documentations while development and testing. I took the responsibility as the SCRUM Master and the curator to manage all submissions, documentations, and the Kanboard. We developed the homepage along with 11 steps that are to be completed before the first day of classes. Our project was very heavy on data and required consistent meetings with the client to fetch all information and get feedback on previous sprint's deliverables. Needless to say, we had a smooth ride throughout the duration of the project.

We divided our features into "Must Have", Nice to have" and "Good To have" to prioritize the project and consolidate the most important features to the 8-week time period. SCRUM really helped us in tracking our lessons learned and having consistent communication to maintain our project's deliverables and timeline. We faced a few obstacles, but Agile and SCRUM helped us push through and improve our mistakes. It was a wonderful journey that let us get a good insight in the Project Management world!
