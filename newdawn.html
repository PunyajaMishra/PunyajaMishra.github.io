About the Project

This project is undertaken by Trent University Enactus to develop a platform for assisting the homeless population. As a part of the University's Enactus Club, I came up with this Project Idea to revive the conditions of the homeless and take a step towards No Poverty. The Project “New Dawn” aims to create a platform that will act as a census of the Homeless Population for the public to access and aid the population.

This platform will be a Web Application that will allow the general public to create an account on the Web Application, add details about the homeless person they saw using geolocation services and comment if they helped a particular homeless person. This will give this community an identity and the general public, who truly wishes to help, will get a medium to find them and help them. The users will be able to locate a homeless person nearby to help them and also upload some small chores they are willing to hire a homeless person for. This will give an opportunity for the population to stand back on their feet.

There is a security conern when it comes to uploading inromation and location about homeless popilation. Therefore we will be expanding our porject's scope. We will be partnering with other non profit organizations and services like the ones that procide shelters. We would also collaborate with social workers to allow the general public and the social workers to connect with each other and help the homeless. For example, I can find a social worker near me if i have a small "job" for the homeless. This way we consider the security issues regarding the homeless and the general public as well. New Dawn will also come up with services like "Donations" that allows users to donate, of which the entire procedings go towards the homesless

There are many reasons that urban residential areas are deemed as not socially sustainable. It can be either due to the environments conditions of the area or unsanitary reasons(stagnant water/ garbage/ infestations) or the population type residing nearby. Homeless people are often misunderstood and therefore considered unacceptable. This in turn leads to desperate times for them which then calls for desperate measures and therefore lead to an increase in the crime rate in the neighborhood. This project aims to provide opportunities to the population and therefore create a more socially sustainable urban residential buildings.
