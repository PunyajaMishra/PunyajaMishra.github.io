ABOUT ME

Punyaja Mishra is an aspiring data scientist who strongly believes data can be used to make better and efficient decisions that can help not only a business but also a person grow. Analysing is the way of world to improve and grow. Punyaja has always had strong critical & analytical skills, good grasp on Mathematics and, finding trends and patterns that others might miss. She considers that being a data analyst is like tracking and analysing clues within data & numbers to find the aspect that would be profitable. As a Computer Science Honors graduate with a specialization in Software Engineering & Data Analysis, she wants to use this background along with her natural skills to perform well in Data Science.

Punyaja has always been an organized person that can carry out multiple tasks smoothly and also well under pressure. A beautiful example for this is her time as a University student, where along with managing the immense load of the Courses and leading 3 group projects, she also managed to use her hours in multiple jobs to support herself. During her time as a student, she grabbed the opportunity to work as a Software Developer for the startup "InnovFin Consulting Inc" under the CEO Othalia Doe-Bruce herself for more than half a year. There, she not only got a chance to work with Advance Technologies like Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality, but also made important decisions for the company future alongside the CEO. After this wonderful experience, she got a good 1 year of exposure in the Release Management Team of the IT Portfolio Management at "Toronto Hydro". This was a fascinating transition from a developer to learn about the further processes that takes place after the software development in the SDLC (Sotware Development LifeCycle).

In her final year she got a chance to build a Web Application Dashboard for a real-life client "Longworth Dental Boutique" as a part of her Capstone Project. The project gave her an exposure as a Full stack developer, project manager & Data Analyst. Here, explored Web development tools (HTML, CSS, Php, JS), Relational Database Tools (SQL) and Data Visualization Principles all under Agile Methodology on Atlassian Jira.
